## Bill Gates Style Questions

This section collects some weird questions along the lines of the [Manhole Cover Question](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_interview#Manhole_cover_question).

* What would happen if you put a mirror in a scanner? [[A]](https://gizmodo.com/what-would-happen-if-you-put-a-mirror-in-a-scanner-1693666872)
* Imagine there's a perfect clone of yourself. Imagine that that clone is your boss. Would you like to work for him/her?
* Interview me
* Why are Quora's answers better than Yahoo Answers' ones? [[A]](https://www.quora.com/What-are-the-differences-between-Quora-Reddit-and-Yahoo-Answers)
* Let's play a game: defend Cobol against modern languages, and try to find as many reasonable arguments as you can. [[A]](https://blog.microfocus.com/cobol-the-language-for-future-generations/)
* Where will you be in 10 years?
* You are my boss and I'm fired. Inform me.
* I want to refactor a legacy system. You want to rewrite it from scratch. Argument. Then, switch our roles. [[A]](https://softwareengineering.stackexchange.com/questions/226534/how-to-convince-prove-my-manager-that-a-rewriting-is-needed-rather-than-a-refact)
* Your boss asks you to lie to the Company. What's your reaction?
* If you could travel back in time, which advice would you give to your younger self?
